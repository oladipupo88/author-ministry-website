Why I Write

I write because I believe the Word of God is the most powerful force for human transformation available on earth. This platform exists as the written expression of a conviction shaped through years of pastoral ministry: that disciples are not formed by inspiration alone, but by the patient, faithful and accurate teaching of the whole counsel of God.

AUTHOR BIOGRAPHY

The Pastor Behind The Library

Dr. Jacob Kehinde Oladipupo is the Senior Pastor of Great Commission Christian Church (GCCC), San Antonio, Texas, which he planted in 2018.

He holds a Doctor of Philosophy (Ph.D.) in Missions and Evangelism from Southwestern Baptist Theological Seminary, Fort Worth, Texas, and a Doctor of Philosophy (Ph.D.) in Biblical Studies and Hermeneutics from Newburgh Theological Seminary.

As chaplain, he completed five units of Clinical Pastoral Education (CPE) at Baptist Health System, San Antonio, developing a deeper understanding of pastoral care and ministry presence.

The Writing Ministry

The Jacob K. Oladipupo Library contains published and unpublished works covering biblical exposition, pastoral theology, discipleship, Christian leadership, marriage, family and evangelism.

Each book combines theological depth with practical application, written for pastors, leaders, students, missionaries and everyday believers who desire to grow deeper in Christ.

"Every book in this library was written with a conviction: the Word of God, faithfully opened, has the power to build what nothing else can build — and to free what nothing else can free."

Dr. Jacob Kehinde Oladipupo

Family & Personal Life
